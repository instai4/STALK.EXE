## 🌟 Project Overview

Ever wondered what truly makes a developer tick? Beyond the code, there's a fascinating personality waiting to be discovered! **STALK.EXE** is a super cool web app designed to do just that. It dives deep into public GitHub data to conjure up a **"psychological profile"** of any developer you point it to. Think of it as your digital detective for understanding the human behind the commits! 🕵️‍♀️💻

It's not just about lines of code; it's about identifying patterns, analyzing personalities, peeking into their "dark side" (all in good fun, of course!), predicting future endeavors, and delivering a comprehensive verdict. All powered by cutting-edge AI!

---

## ✨ Key Features

Get ready to uncover some seriously interesting insights! STALK.EXE comes packed with these awesome capabilities:

*   **🎯 Identify Target:** Simply plug in a GitHub username or repository URL, and let the hunt begin!
*   **🧠 Analyze Personality:** Discover the core traits and characteristics reflected in their coding style and contributions.
*   **😈 Dark Side Unveiled:** A playful (and anonymous!) look into potential quirks or hidden developer habits.
*   **🛠️ Stack Tech Mastery:** Get a clear picture of their preferred programming languages, frameworks, and tools.
*   **🔮 Future Prediction:** Glimpse into potential future projects, career paths, or areas of growth.
*   **⚖️ The Grand Verdict:** A summarized psychological file, giving you a unique perspective on the developer.
*   **🚀 Powered by AI:** Leveraging Groq and Gemini Vision APIs for intelligent analysis and insights!

## 🚀 How to Use

It's ridiculously simple to get your detective hat on and start exploring!

1.  **Visit the Live App:** Head over to our live demo link: [https://stalk-exe.vercel.app/](https://stalk-exe.vercel.app/)
2.  **Enter GitHub Info:** In the input field, type in:
    *   A **GitHub Username** (e.g., `instai4`)
    *   Or a **GitHub Repository URL** (e.g., `https://github.com/instai4/STALK.EXE`)
3.  **Hit Enter/Analyze:** Press the enter key or click the "Analyze" button.
4.  **Voila!** Watch as STALK.EXE fetches data and crafts a fascinating psychological file for you!
